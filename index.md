# DynaSpeak Placement Test — Study Guide

Welcome! This guide will help you prepare for the **DynaSpeak English placement test** in Auckland, New Zealand.

---

## What is the placement test?

DynaSpeak is a free English language school run by Te Wananga o Aotearoa. Before you join a class, you take a short placement test so the teachers can find the **right level class** for you.

> This is not a pass or fail test. There are no wrong answers - just honest ones. The test helps you get into the best class for your level.

---

## The 5 levels

| Level | Name | Description |
|-------|------|-------------|
| 1 | Beginner | Little or no English |
| 2 | Elementary | Basic greetings and simple sentences |
| 3 | Pre-intermediate | Can talk about familiar topics |
| 4 | Intermediate | Can hold a conversation on most topics |
| 5 | Upper-intermediate | Can read, write and speak with confidence |

---

## What does the test cover?

1. Grammar and vocabulary
2. Reading
3. Speaking
4. Writing
