# DynaSpeak Placement Test — Study Guide

Welcome! This guide will help you prepare for the **DynaSpeak English placement test** in Auckland, New Zealand.

---

## What is the placement test?

DynaSpeak is a free English language school run by Te Wānanga o Aotearoa. Before you join a class, you take a short placement test so the teachers can find the **right level class** for you.

> ✅ **This is not a pass or fail test.** There are no wrong answers — just honest ones. The test helps you get into the best class for your level.

---

## The 5 levels

| Level | Name | Description |
|-------|------|-------------|
| 1 | Beginner | Little or no English |
| 2 | Elementary | Basic greetings and simple sentences |
| 3 | Pre-intermediate | Can talk about familiar topics |
| 4 | Intermediate | Can hold a conversation on most topics |
| 5 | Upper-intermediate | Can read, write and speak with confidence |

---

## What does the test cover?

The placement test assesses four skill areas:

1. **Grammar & vocabulary** — sentence structure and word knowledge
2. **Reading** — short passage with comprehension questions
3. **Speaking** — a friendly conversation with a teacher
4. **Writing** — a short paragraph about yourself or your life

## Tips for the test

- Speak slowly and clearly in the speaking section
- If you don't understand a question, ask: *"Sorry, can you please repeat that?"*
- In the writing section, check capital letters and full stops
- In the reading section, read the questions **before** you read the passage
- Be honest — the test is there to help you, not judge you
