# Quiz 2 — Vocabulary

**Covers:** Lesson 6 (everyday vocabulary)  
**Questions:** 15  
**Instructions:** Choose the best answer. Answers are at the bottom.

---

**1.** Which word means "the country you come from"?

- a) occupation  
- b) residence  
- c) nationality  
- d) experience  

---

**2.** Choose the best word:  
*I do household _____ every morning — cooking, cleaning, and washing.*

- a) commutes  
- b) chores  
- c) budgets  
- d) resources  

---

**3.** Which sentence uses "because" correctly?

- a) I study English because I want to find a job.  
- b) I study English because I also want to.  
- c) Because I study English.  
- d) I study English because however.  

---

**4.** Choose the correct linking word:  
*I like Auckland. _____, I miss my family.*

- a) Because  
- b) Also  
- c) However  
- d) So that  

---

**5.** What does "fluent" mean?

- a) You are learning a language slowly.  
- b) You cannot speak a language.  
- c) You speak a language very well and naturally.  
- d) You are translating a language.  

---

**6.** Choose the best word:  
*My husband _____ to work by train every day.*

- a) commutes  
- b) tidies  
- c) budgets  
- d) enrolls  

---

**7.** Which sentence uses a sequencing word correctly?

- a) First, I wake up. Finally, I make breakfast. Then, I go to work.  
- b) First, I wake up. Then, I make breakfast. Finally, I go to work.  
- c) Finally, I wake up. First, I go to work. Then, I make breakfast.  
- d) Then, I wake up. Also, I go to work. However, I make breakfast.  

---

**8.** What does "grateful" mean?

- a) angry about something  
- b) thankful for something  
- c) worried about something  
- d) confused about something  

---

**9.** Choose the correct word:  
*I am _____ in New Zealand — sometimes I miss my family and friends in my country.*

- a) excited  
- b) confident  
- c) homesick  
- d) motivated  

---

**10.** Which phrase is used to give a reason?

- a) However  
- b) Also  
- c) Because  
- d) Finally  

---

**11.** Choose the best sentence:  
*I am studying English _____ I can communicate better with my children's teachers.*

- a) although  
- b) so that  
- c) however  
- d) in addition  

---

**12.** What does "neighbourhood" mean?

- a) a large shopping centre  
- b) the area around your home  
- c) a type of transport  
- d) a community meeting  

---

**13.** Choose the best word:  
*I am _____ to improve my English this year — I study every day.*

- a) worried  
- b) unmotivated  
- c) motivated  
- d) bored  

---

**14.** Which sentence uses "also" correctly?

- a) I speak Tagalog. I also speak some English.  
- b) Also I speak Tagalog and English.  
- c) I speak Tagalog also and English.  
- d) I also but speak English.  

---

**15.** What does "enrol" mean in this context?  
*"I want to enrol in an English class."*

- a) finish  
- b) pay for  
- c) register or sign up for  
- d) leave  

---

## ✅ Answer key

| Q | Answer | Note |
|---|--------|------|
| 1 | **c) nationality** | "The country you come from" |
| 2 | **b) chores** | Household tasks |
| 3 | **a) I study English because I want to find a job.** | "Because" introduces a reason |
| 4 | **c) However** | "However" introduces a contrast |
| 5 | **c) You speak a language very well and naturally.** | Definition of "fluent" |
| 6 | **a) commutes** | Travel to work regularly |
| 7 | **b) First, I wake up. Then, I make breakfast. Finally, I go to work.** | Correct time sequence |
| 8 | **b) thankful for something** | Definition of "grateful" |
| 9 | **c) homesick** | Missing your home country |
| 10 | **c) Because** | "Because" = reason |
| 11 | **b) so that** | "So that" = purpose / reason for doing something |
| 12 | **b) the area around your home** | Definition of "neighbourhood" |
| 13 | **c) motivated** | Wanting to do something and working for it |
| 14 | **a) I speak Tagalog. I also speak some English.** | Correct use of "also" |
| 15 | **c) register or sign up for** | "Enrol" = join / register |
