## ✅ Answer key

| Q | Answer | Explanation |
|---|--------|-------------|
| 1 | **b) is** | She = he/she/it → use "is" |
| 2 | **d) He is not at home.** | Correct verb form for "he" |
| 3 | **a) They're happy.** | Correct contraction of "they are" |
| 4 | **c) Are** | "you" takes "are" |
| 5 | **d) Both a and b are correct** | Both "she isn't" and "she's not" are correct |
| 6 | **b) cooks** | "My mother" = she → add -s |
| 7 | **c) She doesn't like coffee.** | After "doesn't", use base verb (no -s) |
| 8 | **c) Does** | "he" uses "does" not "do" |
| 9 | **c) go** | "I" uses base verb in present simple |
| 10 | **b) always** | "always" fits a present simple habit; "yesterday" and "ago" are past tense time words |
| 11 | **c) are playing** | Present continuous: are + verb-ing |
| 12 | **c) She is studying at the moment.** | Correct form: is + studying |
| 13 | **b) isn't watching** | Present continuous negative |
| 14 | **d) went** | Irregular past of "go" |
| 15 | **b) She didn't go to school.** | After "didn't", use base verb |
| 16 | **c) worked** | Regular past tense of "work" |
| 17 | **c) bought** | Irregular past of "buy" |
| 18 | **b) an / a** | "an" before vowel sound (apple); "a" before consonant sound (biscuit) |
| 19 | **b) Children need love.** | General plural noun — no article needed |
| 20 | **c) the** | Specific door — both speaker and listener know which one |
