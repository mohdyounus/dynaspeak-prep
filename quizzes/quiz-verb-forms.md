**Instructions:** Choose the best answer. Answers are at the bottom.

## Section A - Verb forms

**1.** Which word is the past participle of **do**?

- a) did
- b) done
- c) doing
- d) does

**2.** Which sentence is correct?

- a) She has went home.
- b) She has gone home.
- c) She is gone home.
- d) She going home.

**3.** Which form is used in continuous tenses?

- a) base form
- b) past participle
- c) -ing form
- d) infinitive only

**4.** Choose the correct sentence.

- a) I have did my homework.
- b) I did my homework yesterday.
- c) I done my homework yesterday.
- d) I am did my homework.

**5.** Which line is correct?

- a) be - was - been - being
- b) be - been - was - being
- c) be - were - being - beened
- d) be - is - wased - being

## Answer key

| Q | Answer | Explanation |
|---|--------|-------------|
| 1 | b) done | **done** is the past participle of **do** |
| 2 | b) She has gone home. | after **has**, use past participle |
| 3 | c) -ing form | continuous tenses use verb-ing |
| 4 | b) I did my homework yesterday. | simple past uses **did** |
| 5 | a) be - was - been - being | correct verb family order |
| 6 | c) are studying | action happening now |
| 7 | b) sent | finished action in the past |
| 8 | d) will go | future with **will + base verb** |
| 9 | c) I cook dinner every evening. | a repeated routine |
| 10 | c) yesterday | common past simple signal word |
| 11 | b) has | **she** takes **has** |
| 12 | c) have | **we** takes **have** |
| 13 | a) has been | present perfect with **he** |
| 14 | b) had been | earlier past before another past action |
| 15 | c) We had lunch at noon. | correct past form |
| 16 | b) being | after **for**, use **being** here |
| 17 | c) done | after **have**, use past participle |
| 18 | b) been | **has been** is correct |
| 19 | b) did | simple past main verb |
| 20 | b) He is being noisy today. | present continuous form of **be** |
