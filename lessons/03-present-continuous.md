# Lesson 3 — Present continuous tense

**Level:** Elementary  
**Time:** 15–20 minutes  
**Skills:** Grammar, Speaking

---

## What you will learn

- How to talk about things happening **right now**
- How to form the present continuous
- The difference between present simple and present continuous

---

## When do we use the present continuous?

| Use | Example |
|-----|---------|
| Something happening right now | *I am studying English now.* |
| Something happening around this period | *She is working at DynaSpeak this month.* |
| A temporary situation | *They are staying with their cousin.* |

**Time words** for present continuous:
> now / right now / at the moment / currently / today / this week / this month

---

## Part 1 — Positive sentences

**Structure:** Subject + **am / is / are** + verb**-ing**

| Subject | am/is/are | verb-ing | Example |
|---------|-----------|----------|---------|
| I | am | working | I **am working** right now. |
| You | are | studying | You **are studying** English. |
| He | is | cooking | He **is cooking** dinner. |
| She | is | reading | She **is reading** a book. |
| It | is | raining | It **is raining** outside. |
| We | are | learning | We **are learning** grammar. |
| They | are | playing | They **are playing** at the park. |

### Spelling rules for -ing

| Rule | Examples |
|------|---------|
| Most verbs: just add **-ing** | work → working, eat → eating, play → playing |
| Verbs ending in **-e**: remove e, add **-ing** | make → making, live → living, write → writing |
| Short verbs ending in consonant-vowel-consonant: double the last letter | run → running, sit → sitting, swim → swimming |

---

## Part 2 — Negative sentences

**Structure:** Subject + **am not / isn't / aren't** + verb-ing

| Example |
|---------|
| I **am not watching** TV. / I'm not watching TV. |
| He **isn't working** today. |
| They **aren't studying** right now. |

---

## Part 3 — Questions

**Structure:** **Am / Is / Are** + subject + verb-ing?

| Question | Short answer (yes) | Short answer (no) |
|----------|--------------------|-------------------|
| **Are** you studying? | Yes, I am. | No, I'm not. |
| **Is** she cooking? | Yes, she is. | No, she isn't. |
| **Are** they playing? | Yes, they are. | No, they aren't. |

### Wh- questions

> *What **are** you **doing**?*  
> *Where **is** he **going**?*  
> *Why **are** they **laughing**?*

---

## Part 4 — Present simple vs present continuous

This is a common area of confusion. Here is how to choose:

| Present simple | Present continuous |
|----------------|-------------------|
| Habits and routines | Right now / temporary |
| *I **work** every day.* (= my job, my routine) | *I **am working** right now.* (= at this moment) |
| *She **lives** in Auckland.* (= permanent home) | *She **is staying** with a friend.* (= temporary) |
| *He **plays** football on Saturdays.* (= habit) | *He **is playing** football now.* (= right now) |

---

## Vocabulary for this lesson

| Word | Meaning | Example |
|------|---------|---------|
| currently | at this time | I am currently studying. |
| temporary | not permanent | This is a temporary job. |
| permanent | lasting a long time | We have a permanent home. |
| at the moment | right now | I'm busy at the moment. |

## ✏️ Practice exercise

Fill in the gaps with the present continuous form:

1. I _____ (write) a letter to my family.
2. She _____ (not / watch) TV right now.
3. _____ they _____ (come) to class today?
4. My children _____ (play) in the garden.
5. It _____ (rain) heavily outside.
6. We _____ (learn) English this week.
7. He _____ (not / work) today — it is a holiday.
8. What _____ you _____ (do) right now?

**Answers:** 1. am writing 2. is not watching / isn't watching 3. Are / coming 4. are playing 5. is raining 6. are learning 7. is not working / isn't working 8. are / doing
