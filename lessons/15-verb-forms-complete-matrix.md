## MASTER REFERENCE TABLE: All Verb Forms at a Glance

### PRESENT SIMPLE

| Subject | Positive | Negative | Question |
|---------|----------|----------|----------|
| I | I **work** | I **don't work** | **Do** I work? |
| You | You **work** | You **don't work** | **Do** you work? |
| He/She/It | He **works** | He **doesn't work** | **Does** he work? |
| We | We **work** | We **don't work** | **Do** we work? |
| They | They **work** | They **don't work** | **Do** they work? |

**Use for:** habits, routines, facts  
**Time words:** every day, usually, always, never, often

---

### PRESENT PERFECT (with HAVE)

| Subject | Positive | Negative | Question |
|---------|----------|----------|----------|
| I | I **have worked** | I **have not worked** | **Have** I worked? |
| You | You **have worked** | You **have not worked** | **Have** you worked? |
| He/She/It | He **has worked** | He **has not worked** | **Has** he worked? |
| We | We **have worked** | We **have not worked** | **Have** we worked? |
| They | They **have worked** | They **have not worked** | **Have** they worked? |

**Contraction:** have not = haven't, has not = hasn't  
**Use for:** experience, recent past, or situation continuing until now  
**Time words:** ever, never, already, yet, just, for two years, since 2024

## SPECIAL VERBS: BE, HAVE, DO

### The verb BE (present)

| Subject | Positive | Negative | Question |
|---------|----------|----------|----------|
| I | I **am** | I **am not** | **Am** I? |
| You | You **are** | You **are not** | **Are** you? |
| He/She/It | He **is** | He **is not** | **Is** he? |
| We | We **are** | We **are not** | **Are** we? |
| They | They **are** | They **are not** | **Are** they? |

---

### The verb BE (past)

| Subject | Positive | Negative | Question |
|---------|----------|----------|----------|
| I | I **was** | I **was not** | **Was** I? |
| You | You **were** | You **were not** | **Were** you? |
| He/She/It | He **was** | He **was not** | **Was** he? |
| We | We **were** | We **were not** | **Were** we? |
| They | They **were** | They **were not** | **Were** they? |

---

### The verb HAVE (possession)

| Subject | Present | Past | Note |
|---------|---------|------|------|
| I / you / we / they | **have** | **had** | Same past form for all |
| he / she / it | **has** | **had** | Same past form for all |

---

### The verb DO (in questions and negatives)

| Tense | Do/Does/Did | Base verb | Example |
|-------|------------|-----------|---------|
| Present I/you/we/they | **do** | base | **Do** you work? |
| Present he/she/it | **does** | base | **Does** she work? |
| Past all subjects | **did** | base | **Did** they work? |

## QUICK DECISION CHART

**When do I use which tense?**

| Question | Answer | Tense |
|----------|--------|-------|
| Is it happening RIGHT NOW? | Yes | Present continuous |
| Do I do this usually/every day? | Yes | Present simple |
| Did it happen and finish? | Yes, in the past | Past simple |
| Was it happening while something else happened? | Yes | Past continuous |
| Have I ever...? | Never/always/just | Present perfect |
| What happened BEFORE another past event? | Past event before another | Past perfect |
| Will it happen later? | Yes | Will / going to |
| Am I planning to do it? | Yes, I have a plan | Going to |
| Will I decide now? | Yes, deciding now | Will |

## Common mistakes

**Mistake 1: Mixing positive simple and continuous**  
❌ *She work now.*  
✅ *She **is working** now.*

**Mistake 2: Forgetting to change the verb for he/she/it**  
❌ *She **work** every day.*  
✅ *She **works** every day.*

**Mistake 3: Using wrong form after have/has**  
❌ *I have **go** home.*  
✅ *I have **gone** home.*

**Mistake 4: Saying "do" instead of "doing" in continuous**  
❌ *They are **do** homework.*  
✅ *They are **doing** homework.*

## Practice: Choose the correct form

1. _____ you work tomorrow? (Will / Are)
2. She _____ her homework every evening. (do / does)  
3. They _____ listening to music right now. (are / is)
4. I _____ already finished my meal. (have / has)
5. We _____ the movie when it started raining. (watch / were watching)

**Answers:** 
1. Will
2. does
3. are
4. have
5. were watching
